About Us

Our group is an interdisciplinary research consortium made up of plant geneticists , soil microbiologists and computational biologists.

Plant Genetics

Our focus is characterizing the genotypic-phenotypic interaction between Sorghum bioclor and its rhizosphere. We are investigating how sb geneotype influences the make up of its rhizosphere

Soil Microbiology

We are using high throughput 2D and 3D microscopy to characterize the diveristy and richness of AM fungi in the rhizhosphere of SB

Computational Biology

We design and build computational tools to model and annotate the symbiotic relationship between AM fungi and SB

Our studies are generously funded by the Department of Energy and the National Science foundation. If you wish to join our team or contribute to our research please feel free to reach out below If you use our software or our data in your study please cite the researcher's work you used in your project.
